Avoid the Spikeballs and collect Stars for the highest score possible in this exciting arcade game!

The object of the game is to collect Stars as you avoid the Spikeballs raining down from the sky. You can also use your abilities to defend against the Spikeballs. Mix and match abilities to best suit your gameplay style! You can use the Stars you collect in-game to unlock new characters, abilities and more!

Compete against your friends for the highest score possible with online leaderboards via Google Play Games Services!

Features:
  • 11 Playable characters
  • Over 20 hats to collect to customize your character
  • 4 stages with increasing difficulty
  • 6 different abilities to use in-game
  • Online Leaderboards via Google Play Games Services
