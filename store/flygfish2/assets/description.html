Guide Flygfish through the depths of the ocean on his quest to collect kelp in three different addicting game modes!

Flygfish 2: Swimtastic is an entertaining way to pass the time wherever you might be!

Features Include:
  • Three different game modes (Arcade, Classic, and Extreme)
  • Achievements to unlock
  • Fun and addicting gameplay
  • Charming visuals
  • An exit button ;)
