WB Arcade features 10 fun filled multiplayer games that are played locally on a single device. Games range from 2 to 6 players.

Bring your friends and/or family together to play! Perfect for a long car ride or while waiting for your flight or even a party!

Games include:
  • Mount Mayhem (2-4 Players)
  • Fruit Math (2 Players)
  • Birds vs Bats (2 Players)
  • Bombs 'n' Shields (3-6 Players)
  • Guess The Time... (2-4 Players)
  • React Fast! (2-6 Players)
  • Swipe Soccer (2 Players)
  • Flygfish (2 Players)
  • Pop The Peach (2 Players)
  • Takamals Battle Arena (2 Players)

You will earn tokens from playing games, which can be exchanged for tickets in the Prize Center. Tickets can then be exchanged for various prizes such as new profile pictures! There are over 70 profile pictures and over 50 other unlockables to unlock!

Warning: This game involves sharing your device with others, it is recommended that you turn off windowed/pop-up notifications to prevent disruptions during game play.
