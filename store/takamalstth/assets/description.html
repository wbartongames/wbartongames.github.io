When everlasting darkness threatens all worlds, you must take on the challenge of collecting all 12 Eternal Hearts and become a hero. Takamals: Tale of Twelve Hearts is an RPG and Strategy game with both unique and familiar mechanics. Play through 12 Chapters of a story while battling and recruiting new Takamals.

Features:
  • 90 unique Takamals
  • 12 Chapter Story (~30 min per Chapter)
  • Adventure Log to keep track of story progress
  • New items and battle system
  • Cross-Platform Save File transferring between devices
