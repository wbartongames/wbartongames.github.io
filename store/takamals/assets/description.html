Embark on an unforgettable adventure in Takamals!

Play through three different modes:

Story Mode:

Follow the charming story of a kid who befriends an unfortunate scientific experiment, "Stompy", and ends up saving the world from the evil Wrathful Corporations. Befriend new Takamals along your journey and battle your way to greatness. With many new areas to discover, this 4+ hour story has a lot to offer!

Battle Arena:

Choose from three randomly generated Takamals and try to defeat as many Takamals as you can without losing. Be careful though, each Arena gets harder and harder each time!

Zoo Mode:

Open card packs to expand your Zoo. Keep expanding your Zoo in order to get as much money as possible!

Features Include:
  • Unique Artstyle
  • Unique Game Mechanics
  • Unique Battle System
  • Developed Story
  • Over 40 Takamals
  • Fun for all ages
